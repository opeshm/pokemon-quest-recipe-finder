Overview

PokeQuest Studio is a fan-made reference app for Pokemon Quest recipes, moves, and Pokemon data. The app is designed to work without user accounts, forms, or personal profiles.

Information We Collect

This app does not ask you to provide personal information. It does not include account registration, comments, contact forms, or payment flows.

Local App Data

Filters and selected recipes may appear in the page URL so views can be shared or restored. This data stays in your browser unless you choose to share the URL.

Third-Party Services

The app loads its recipe dataset and static assets from the same site. It does not intentionally send personal information to third-party analytics or advertising services.

Changes

This policy may be updated when the app changes. The updated date above will reflect the latest version.
